A Brief History of Ronald McDonald

Who is Ronald McDonald?

Ronald McDonald is an actor, mascot, philosopher, philanthropist, venture capitalist and is also well-known an "S Tier" character on the Super Smash Bros. tier list.

Ronald McDonald - an ancient, mythological beastkin, or mortal man, in a clown costume?

Where did Ronald McDonald come from?

Ronald McDonald (age unknown) was first sighted on July 4th, 1963 in Henderson, Nebraska, by a corn farmer who heard unsettling murmurs coming from nearby his grain silos. After inspection, the farmer found Ronald McDonald hunched over a moderately sized pile of raw corn cobs, feverishly gnawing on them. Ronald McDonald, becoming alerted by the farmers presence, fled the scene whilst producing a high-pitched screeching sound.

The farmer later described Ronald McDonald as having "souless eyes, like those of something demonic" and being "erratic and disturbed in nature". Some theorists believe Ronald McDonald is an inter-dimensional being, capable of travelling through time and space at will.

Physical Characteristics

Ronald McDonald has voluntarily particpated in numerous scientific studies. Hawking, et al have described Ronald McDonald as having "the perfect metabolic system" whereby his physical body adapts with great ease to internal and external factors. Due to this, he is able to:

  • Consume very little food or water and survive for months at a time
  • Breathe underwater and in space
  • Never sleep
